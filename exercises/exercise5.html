History

When I was little, I actually didn’t enjoy reading. It wasn’t until I was about halfway through middle school that I really started to enjoy reading and began reading outside of what was required for my classes (though I often didn’t even read those assigned stories). I began reading prodigiously, and for much of my schooling from middle school through college, you could often find me reading a book now mostly on Kindle, but previously books I would check out from the library.

Programming, unlike reading, I hadn’t discovered until later in life. rowing up, I had limited access to technology, and being in a rural area, programming classes weren’t available in school. I only truly got into programming later in high school and into college where I began pursuing an education in it, beginning with a few classes and finding myself truly enjoying it.
